Natural Programming Lab (NPL) is led by Prof. Yewen Pu at Nanyang Technological University. We study how agentic AI can align with humans through interaction—beyond settings with crisp goals and immediate feedback—with emphasis on communication-driven intelligence (e.g., iterative repair, convention formation, shared mental models) and on systems that are natural to instruct and reliable in practice.
