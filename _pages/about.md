We study how to bridge the gap between today’s agentic systems and true human collaborators. While modern LLMs excel at tasks with clear objectives and feedback, many real-world problems require alignment through interaction. Our work focuses on communication-driven intelligence—such as iterative repair, convention formation, and the development of shared mental models—and builds interactive systems that are both natural to instruct and reliable in execution.
